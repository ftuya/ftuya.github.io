Fernando Tuya
Associate Professor (“Profesor Titular”)
Head of the Biodiversity and Conservation (BIOCON) group, IU-ECOAQUA, University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria (ULPGC), Canary Islands, Spain.
BIOCON @ IU-ECOAQUA

Coastal community ecologist working across the globe’s oceans, particularly on nearshore habitats underpinned by foundation species (seagrasses, macroalgae, rhodoliths) and on macroecology, biogeography and the sustainable management of marine ecosystems.
