Science dissemination

Popular science materials on the marine biodiversity of the Canary Islands and Macaronesia.

I seek to transfer knowledge on marine biodiversity of the Canary Islands and Macaronesia to the wider public through popular books and booklets, covering topics such as species identification and the basic ecology of nearshore habitats.
